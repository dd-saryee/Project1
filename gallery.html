Our Church

Bible Teaching Ministry located headquater located in Monrovia at 12th Street. Come let us worship and praise our creator together every sunday from 8am to 3pm.
Our Sunday services are divide into two bashes. The first from 8am to 11am and the second from 1pm to 3pm.Choose the one that you think is suitable for you and take part.
