Welcome to Bible Teaching Ministry

We help you to be in the presence of God everyday through our daily and weekly programs. Our pastor receives members everyday from 8am to 12pm, except from sunday. The church has a lot of activities, we host a crusade every end of month where we invite powerful man of God from all parts of the world to share the word of God.
We always have two services on Sundays, one from 8am to 11am and the other starts from 1pm and ends by 3pm.
God uses our Bishop to perform many miracles and healings in the church. Whatever it is that you are suffering from, come to Bible Teaching Ministry God will heal you.
